DES16 - poster

Winter - 2018
Tools: Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator

These are poster projects I did on DES16. The left one is about a specific character which we selected ourselves. I chose my favorite painter Vincent Van Gogh. His passion about oil painting and his style inspired me a lot. The other one is a typeface poster. And the typeface I chose is Garamond. DES14 - Storyboard

Summer1 - 2018
Tools: Watercolor, ink

This is the final project of DES14, the travel Storyboard. The purpose of this project is showing beauty of a specific city to foreigners. I chose the capital of China, Beijing. The six different panels are all showing the most famous food or places of Beijing for travellers.
